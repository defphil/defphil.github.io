Hi, my name is Filip

I am student interested in functional and low level programming. Currently I study Information Systems and Technologies at Faculty of Organizational Science, University of Belgrade. Previously I was majoring Electrical Engineering for 2 years.

I am mostly interested in Clojure and Rust. I had experience with Java, C++, C, Scheme, Assembly and Python. My first programming language was TurboPascal, during early highschool days. I consider myself operating systems nerd and UNIX enthusiast (using illumos on my home server). I like learning about computers all over the stack, from hardware design and OS kernel all the way up to application and web programming. I’ve been doing some creative coding recently just for fun.

I also love art, reading, listening to music and DJing, riding skate and hanging out in nature.

In the end it’s all about having fun and not being bored. I hate being bored. Feel free to get in touch.
